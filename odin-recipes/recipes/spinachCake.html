Vanilla Spinach Cake

Description

This Vanilla Spinach Cake is bright green, but the flavour of the spinach fades away so it just tastes like a vanilla cake - but it has an incredible colour!

It's a fun hidden vegetable cake, that's delicious and slightly healthier.

Perfect as a birthday cake or for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 3/4 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 3/4 cup of vegetable oil
  • 1 cup of spinach puree
  • 25 g of baking powder
  • Vanilla extract
  • 2.5 cups of flour

Steps

  1. Cream together eggs and sugar until white and frothy
  2. Add the milk, vanilla extract and vegetable oil and whisk
  3. Add the spinach puree and whisk
  4. Add the flour and baking powder, then stir until you get a smooth batter
  5. Pour into a greased 30 cm cake pan
  6. Bake at 180 degrees Celsius oven until a toothpick inserted in the centre comes out clean
  7. Remove from oven and let cool completely before inverting on a platter
